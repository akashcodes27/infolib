ABOUT US

The largest web developer site on the internet. 3 billion pages displayed each year. 60 million visitors each month. InfoLib was an initiative that was taken by 2 broke college students, who were just about to graduate. The two found it very overwhelming to sustain in the competitive environment, so the two decided to just go for it. The result of us taking the leap of faith back then was a great memorable journey full of ups and down.If the 2 would have instead waited for the right opportunity to show, InfoLib wouldn't have existed today.
